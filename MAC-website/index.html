Our Mission

Our mission is to equip interested members in the Marist community with the relevant knowledge and networking opportunities to work in the field of Data Science.

What sets us Apart

Although Computer Society and Marist Analytics Community (MAC) may parallel each other in a few ways-- our attention is focused towards mathematical modeling, data visualization, database management, artificial intelligence, and machine learning as opposed to sole computer orientation.
